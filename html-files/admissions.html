ADMISSIONS

The University of Nebraska–Lincoln is proud to offer students and families the value of one of the top public university experiences in the U.S. We're ready to help you make the most of the incredible opportunities available in pursuit of your Nebraska degree.

Undergraduate

Academics at Nebraska looks a lot like: close connections with your professors in our 21:1 student-to-faculty ratio classrooms, over 150 majors and minors to pick and choose from, access to research opportunities, study abroad trips, mentorship from our robust alumni network and so, so much more.

International

Ranked in the top third of American public universities, Nebraska enrolls students from more than 100 countries each year. Our strong academic programs are offered in a safe and modern campus environment located in the center of the U.S.A.

Graduate Studies

Expand on your studies with a graduate degree at Nebraska. Our Office of Graduate Studies offers assistance with your admission application, information on funding opportunities, and connects you with faculty members who share your scholarly and research interests.

  • Graduate Studies

College of Law

Nebraska Law develops inclusive leaders in the legal field. A law degree from Nebraska equips students with the tools they need to advance justice, solve problems, and serve with integrity.

  • College of Law

Online & Distance

Through online & distance education, the University of Nebraska– Lincoln offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate courses and programs that provide flexibility while meeting your educational needs.
